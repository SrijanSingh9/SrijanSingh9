About Me

I am a soon-to-be Computer Science graduate from GNDEC, Ludhiana, currently bridging the gap between academia and the tech industry. My work exists at the intersection of robust full-stack engineering—specializing in the Django-React-PostgreSQL ecosystem—and the nuanced realm of artificial intelligence ethics.

I am deeply fascinated by the "black-box" problem in algorithmic governance and strive to build systems rooted in transparency, adhering strictly to Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) principles to ensure technology remains intuitive and accessible.

Beyond algorithms and architecture, my perspective is shaped by a profound appreciation for classic, melancholic poetry and Indian classical music. Whether drafting a research paper on automated fairness or exploring the serene valleys of Uttarakhand, I seek to harmonize technical precision with genuine human expression.
